Our Story

Born From Fire

Hell Out Cafe was born from a passion for authentic charcoal-baked flavors in the heart of Chakan, Maharashtra. We believe great food is made with fire, grit, and soul.

Every pizza that leaves our kitchen has been kissed by real charcoal flames — giving you that unmistakable smoky crust that you simply cannot get from a conventional oven.
