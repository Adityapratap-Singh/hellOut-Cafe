Our Story

Born From Fire

Hell Out Cafe started as a dream — to bring something truly different to the streets of Chakan. We believed that great food shouldn't have to be expensive or inaccessible. It just needed to be made with heart, quality ingredients, and the right heat.

That heat? Real charcoal. Every pizza we bake is fired over an authentic charcoal flame, producing a smoky, crispy crust that simply cannot be replicated by any electric or gas oven. That distinct charcoal flavour is the soul of Hell Out Cafe.

Located at Kad Bandhu Road, Sara City, Chakan — we've grown into a beloved spot for students, families, and food enthusiasts who crave something bold, tasty, and affordable. From our signature Kullad Pizza to our indulgent Maha Raja Burgers, every item on the menu is made fresh daily with pride.
